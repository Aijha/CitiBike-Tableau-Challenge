Citi Bike is a privately owned public bicycle sharing system
 serving the New York City boroughs of the Bronx, Brooklyn, 
 Manhattan, and Queens, as well as Jersey City and Hoboken, 
 New Jersey. Named after lead sponsor Citigroup, it was operated 
 by Motivate (formerly Alta Bicycle Share), with former 
 Metropolitan Transportation Authority CEO Jay Walder as chief 
 executive until September 30, 2018, when the company was acquired
  by Lyft. The system's bikes and stations use technology from Lyft.
